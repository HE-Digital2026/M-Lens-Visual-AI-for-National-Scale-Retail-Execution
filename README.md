# M-Lens — AI-Powered Trade Merchandising for Modern Brands

M-Lens is an advanced trade merchandising and field execution platform designed to give brands total visibility and control over their in-store presence. Powered by AI automation, real-time analytics, and intelligent workflow management, M-Lens transforms traditional retail execution into a fast, accurate, and data-driven process.

Built by **HawkEyes Digital Monitoring Ltd.**, the platform ensures that every outlet visit, every display setup, and every brand asset deployed in the market is captured, verified, and analyzed with precision.

# Core Capabilities

## AI-Based Field Attendance & Verification

M-Lens ensures reliable attendance tracking using facial authentication and live verification to prevent proxy attendance and improve workforce transparency.

### Key Benefits
- Real-time attendance monitoring
- Geo-verified field presence
- Fraud prevention
- Transparent workforce tracking

---

## Smart Route & Visit Planning (PJP Management)

Supervisors can create structured journey plans, assign targets, and monitor territory coverage in real time.

### Features
- Planned Journey Management (PJP)
- Route optimization
- Outlet coverage monitoring
- Visit compliance tracking

## In-Store Image Capture & AI Verification

Field users capture shelf photos, POSM images, and display visuals directly from the mobile application.

M-Lens AI automatically analyzes:

- Brand visibility
- SKU placement
- Share of Shelf (SOS)
- Share of Sachet
- Planogram compliance
- POSM visibility

### Business Impact
- Reduced manual auditing
- Faster retail analysis
- Accurate execution validation
- Improved merchandising compliance
